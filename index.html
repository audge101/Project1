Mind Mixers: A Memory Game

Learn to Play

Welcome! This is a game that will simultaneosly test your memory and build your knowledge of classic cocktail making. You will be given seconds on the clock to memorize cocktail recipes or basic cocktail crafting info, and then you will be expected to recall the recipe to test your memory. There a five levels from the easiest to the hardest.

These levels are:
1. how drinks are served
2. the margarita
3. the old-fashioned
4. the Manhattan
5. the Martini

A perfect score wins the game! Good luck!

Round 5: the Martini

There are numerous ways to make a martini cocktail. This is the classic recipe.

cocktail glass
dash of dry vermouth
2 oz. gin
shake or stir in shaker with ice and strain using a julip strainer
